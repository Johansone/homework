Fettuccine Alfredo

Recipe by Sintija Johansone; someone@somedomain.com.

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccini pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add fettuccini and cook for 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt butter into cream over low heat.
  4. Add salt, pepper and garlic salt. Stir in cheese over medium heat until melted.
  5. Add pasta to sauce. Use enough of the pasta so that all of the sauce is used and the pasta is thoroughly coated.
  6. Serve immediately.
