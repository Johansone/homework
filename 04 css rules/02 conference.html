7th International Conference for Dog Owners

28-30 June 2017, Amsterdam, The Netherlands

IAA (International Association of Animals), ABS (Animal Behavior Society), University of Amsterdam and the Netherlands School Inspectorate invite you to participate in the 7th International Conference of Dogs (ICD-2017). The conference will be held on 28–30 June 2017 at University of Amsterdam (Roeterseiland Campus) in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. We look forward to seeing you in Amsterdam!

Dogs are known as "man's best friend" for a reason. If you're here, you likely know all about how great and loving dogs can be, not to mention helpful and adorable. Owning a dog isn't always all fun and games, though. Dogs need to be properly cared for and can have all sorts of health and behavioral issues. Fortunately, because so many people love dogs, many people have experience with dogs that can benefit others.

Topics this year include:

  • Dogs’ health issues, treatments and insurance
  • Dog training programs and commands
  • Dog breeding regulations
  • And much more!
